About Me

I am Patricio Alejandro Morón, a multidisciplinary artist working across music, photography, writing, and design, with a strong focus on technology, programming, and artificial intelligence. Each creation is a search for new forms of expression, where tradition meets innovation. My goal is to keep innovating, learning from each project, and collaborating with others to expand my horizons, connecting with those who share my passion for the new and the unexpected.
